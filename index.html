About

I'm Mandeep Mann, a Web Developer

I graduated from Juno College of Technology and from Toronto Metropolitan University with a Masters in Early Childhood Studies.

I am someone who enjoys encountering and solving complex problems in code and in games (I love puzzle games!).

As an educator and researcher I am passionate about reflecting on my work and continuing to learn and improve my code to keep up with best practices in accessibility and user experience.

Outside of coding I typically waste away my day binging shows on netflix and playing video games, usually Maplestory or League of Legends, but my favourite game of all time would have to be Portal 2. Some of my other interests include puzzles, anime, and reading.
